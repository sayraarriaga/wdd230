About Us

Bountiful Foods is small business specializing in organic, locally grown foods. We are physically located in Carlsbad, California, however, we have many food trucks that cover the coastal area from Los Angeles to San Diego. Our company sells fruits and produce directly from the farmers in the area, but also uses some national and international suppliers. We want support healthy eating, organic bounty, and convenience
